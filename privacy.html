Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Lexivault ("app", "we"), we value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you use our app and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and username (collected at registration)
  • Game data: Current level, lives count, badges, XP points
  • Purchase information: Subscription status (transaction details are held by App Store / Google Play)
  • Advertising ID: Device advertising ID for interest-based ads via AdMob

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your game progress and achievements
  • Provide subscription services
  • Display in-app ads (free plan users only)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. It is shared only with the following providers for service delivery:

  • Supabase (database and authentication)
  • Google AdMob (ad display)
  • Apple / Google (payment processing)

4. App Permissions

Lexivault requests only internet access permission. No location, camera, microphone, or contacts permissions are requested.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies. AdMob may use your device advertising ID (IDFA/GAID) for ad personalization. You can opt out via your device settings.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored with SSL encryption on Supabase infrastructure. Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures only you can access your own data.

7. Account Deletion

To delete your account, send an email to support@lexivault.app. Your request will be processed within 30 days and all personal data will be deleted.< /p>

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from such users.
