Account Deletion – Lexivault

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to lexivault@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated w ith your Lexivault account.

Your request will be processed within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Game progress: level, XP, badges, lives
  • Subscription and purchase records
  • Username and profile information

Retained Data

Anonymized aggregate statistics (not linked to your identity) may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement purposes, after which they are permanently deleted.

Partial Data Deletion

If you wish to delete only specific data without deleting your account, you may request this via the same email address. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
